About Me

By Chris Holliday

April 5, 2022 - 2 minutes read - 299 words

Hi, I’m Chris, the Cloud Cowboy. By day I’m a Cloud infrastructure architect, with a focus on security, governance and automation. By night… I’m well I guess I’m still that, just more tired.

I work mostly in the Azure cloud. I do some work in AWS, and have certifications in both clouds, but I greatly prefer Azure. My team and I focus on developing guardrails and security controls for our developer community to prevent them from making any big mistakes, and to automatically fix problems where we can. My philosophy is that if what I’m doing doesn’t make my developers able to work faster, safer, and deploy their objects easier, then I’m probably working on the wrong thing and should rethink my approach. But the key is to do ALL three, all the time. We don’t make it easier at the expense of saftey. We make it easier to be safe, and having security built in, makes developement go faster.

Unfortunately, I don’t exactly spend my days in a saddle roping and riding. Instead I spend my time behind a standing desk and a couple of monitors. Instead of a six shooter on my hip, I’ve got a RGB Mechanical Keyboard with 6 programable function keys (and I’m not afraid to use them). Instead of sad songs over a campfire I’ve got endless meetings via Microsoft Teams. Still, the fundamentals of my job is to wrangle all my developers (and management) and keep everybody safe and moving along.

Anyway, this is just a simple blog I put togtether to remind me of things I’ve learned and to help others on their own journey. I’m also hoping this will teach me a thing or 3 about how my developers work as I host this in the cloud myself.

Happy trails
